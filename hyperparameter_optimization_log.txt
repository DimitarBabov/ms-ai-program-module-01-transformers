PART B — WHAT WORKED (in order of impact)
========================================================================
  1. Bidirectional attention + masked mean pooling     (+3% test)
  2. MAX_LENGTH increase (128 -> 384 -> 512)           (+3-4% test)
  3. Length-dependent deletion + trim middle            (+0.04% over uniform)
  4. Random deletion augmentation (uniform or len-dep)  (+0.6% test)
  5. Hidden classification layer                       (+0.32% test)
  6. More epochs (8 -> 12) with augmentation           (+0.26% test)
  7. Full 25k retrain (after finding best epoch)      (+0.38% test)
  8. Cosine LR schedule with warmup
  9. Gradient clipping
  10. Optuna hyperparameter search (PART A)

========================================================================
PART B — WHAT DID NOT WORK
========================================================================
  1. BERT pretrained embeddings (frozen + projection)
  2. 3-layer classification head
  3. Increasing d_embed beyond 192
  4. More than 4 attention heads
  5. No positional encoding (higher val 88.76% but lower test 86.10% vs 86.36%)
